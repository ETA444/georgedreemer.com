thoughtbubble was a knowledge-first social network co-founded by George Dreemer and Nikolay Stanchev between 2014 and 2016 in the United States. It was designed to shift social media away from entertainment toward sharing ideas, insights, and self-improvement content.

The platform grew to around 900 registered users and offered a full social experience — posts, comments, likes, pins, notifications, and customisable profiles — built entirely from scratch by a two-person team.

function notify_author($pid,$actor){
  $author=get_post_author($pid);
  if($author!==$actor){
    insert_notification([
      "recipient"=>$author,
      "type"=>"comment",
      "actor"=>$actor,

}

Element 7 — Full Story Accordion

thoughtbubble started with a simple but ambitious question: what if your social feed made you smarter instead of just killing time? George was 16, living between the Netherlands and the United States, genuinely frustrated with where social media was heading. He and co-founder Nikolay Stanchev decided to build the alternative themselves.

George took full ownership of the engineering. He built the entire backend in PHP and MySQL — every feature from scratch: user registration and login, content posting, a full commenting system, a like engine, pins, a notification system, and customisable profiles. This was not a WordPress install or a template; it was a custom codebase running on Apache, managed through cPanel.

One of the most technically demanding features was the real-time notification engine. Every comment or like triggered a notification to the right user, tracked per recipient, and surfaced in a live badge count — fan-out on write, built before George had any formal computer science education.

thoughtbubble reached ~900 registered users and proved that the product concept had genuine traction. It was eventually wound down, but the experience gave George something no course could: the ability to own an entire technical stack, ship to real users, and solve infrastructure problems under pressure — all before his 18th birthday.
